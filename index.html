About Us

At ByteLab, our mission is to guide young minds into the world of coding through hands-on lessons, real projects, and interactive labs. We believe that the best way to learn programming is by creating, experimenting, and having fun along the way. Each course is carefully designed to build a strong foundation in backend development, databases, and modern technologies like .NET and C#. Whether students dream of becoming developers, engineers, or simply want to understand how technology works, ByteLab helps them take their first confident steps toward a future in tech.

Why Learn to Code?

Coding is more than typing lines on a screen — it’s the language of the future. Every app, game, and website you use was built by someone who knew how to code. It’s the skill that turns ideas into reality. When you learn coding, you learn how to think differently. You don’t just follow instructions — you solve problems, create things from nothing, and make technology work for you. From designing your own website to building smart systems that help people, coding gives you the power to shape the world around you. The earlier you start, the stronger your digital creativity becomes — and the future belongs to creators.

Saffarma

This project is an online drugstore stock management system, a modular backend solution built to power a scalable and maintainable enterprise-level application. Developed using layered Domain-Driven Design (DDD) and clean architecture principles, it efficiently separates concerns across data access, business logic, shared models, and localization resources. The system provides robust and secure RESTful APIs for managing inventory, users, and administrative operations. With integrated localization support and Dockerized deployment, it ensures flexibility, maintainability, and consistency across different environments—making it well-suited for modern DevOps workflows.
